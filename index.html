Mild Everyday Toxicity is
difficult to address

HR lacks tools to address it

  • Usual training risks to backfire because people consider it too politically correct and unimportant
  • Employees usually don't use reporting system for mild everyday toxicity

Our solutions make it easier to address toxicity

Anonymized tools on a mobile app enable employees to fearlessly:

  • Send feedback directly to a toxic colleague.
  • Report to HR about a toxic colleague.

Anonymised feedbacks and reports:

  • are exclusively individual and confidential,
  • can be sent only from within the company,
  • and are subject to our Automated Shields Against Anonymity Abuse.

An HR Console with tools to act on individual cases of mild daily toxicy

  • Simple and powerful investigation tools, ensuring anonymity and confidentiality.
  • Non-disciplinary Cognitive Behavioral tools, and disciplinary tools as a last resort.
