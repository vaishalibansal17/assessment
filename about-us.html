About Us

We created JustEase to respond to the growing toxicity in our society.

We created JustEase to respond to the growing toxicity in our society and and the parallel growing rejection of harassing or discriminating behavior. This evolution has a strong impact on companies, notably via the Great Resignation

We have a deep understanding in how complex organizations function and believe that empowerment and action is key to have a healthy and engaging company culture.

Our solution and the actions we provide are designed to improve communication and empathy in the organization at the grassroots level. While reducing aggression and toxicity, this also improves team performance and productivity thus increasing happiness in the company.

Our team is global from US to France to India. Most of our technology will be in India.
