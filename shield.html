Shields Against Anonymity Abuse

4Shield Against Anonymity Abuse

Anonymity protects senders of feedbacks or reports;

while our Shields protect their colleagues or HR from potential abuse of anonymity, by denying the abuser access to anonymity features.

Shields Against Anonymity Abuse

  • Automated shield against serial sender
  • Automated shield against coordinated feedbacks
  • Shield against abuse of Anonymized report to HR
  • Automated Shield against attack from outside the company

While always providing a safety fall-back solution to the temporarily blocked sender.

4.1Automated shield against
serial sender of Anonymized Feedbacks

Definition of serial sender:
An employee who abusively sends multiple feedbacks to the same or multiple colleagues.

Serial sending could mean one of two situations:

  • An abusive sender
  • or a contributor surrounded by abusive colleagues

In both situations, the employee cannot send any more feedbacks for 3 months.

But the employee can report to HR anonymously or non-anonymously, if they feel surrounded by abusive colleagues.

Automated shield against serial
sender is based on a 2-step algorithm

STEP1If:

Triggers:

A contributor sends feedbacks to 4 colleagues within 2 months.

STEP2 Then:

The Sender:
  • Can't send another feedback during 3 month
  • Is invited to send anonymized Report to HR

4.2Automated shield against coordinated feedbacks

Being several to send abusive feedbacks to someone could seem to guarantee impunity

Coordinated feedbacks:
Many employees coordinating with each other
to send feedback to one employee

It Can Be Either:

Several colleagues sending feedback to the same employee could mean one of two situations

  • An especially abusive contributor
  • A collective complot against some

Both situations need HR intervention. HR is automatically alerted and they can easily and discreetly gauge this employee’s work environment toxicity using the “Targeted Toxicity Survey” feature.

Automated shield against coordinated
feedbacks is based on a 2-step algorithm

STEP1If:

Triggers:

A contributor received feedbacks from
4 colleagues or more within 2 months

STEP2 Then:

The Sender:
  • No more feedbacks are sent to the receiver.
  • HR is informed of trouble in this part of the company.
  • With name of the feedback recipient but not of senders.
  • Suggesting a Targeted Toxicity Survey

4.3Shield Against Abusive Anonymized Reports
to HR

Sending a fake anonymized report to HR might seem tempting and guaranteeing impunity. But the app. gives tools to HR to investigate (Short local toxicity survey, Anonymized chat with reporter...)
...and to sanction abuse by temporarily blocking abuser's access to anonymized features.

But not their access to non-anonymized complain to HR.

If an employee continues to send
unsubstantiated anonymized reports to HR
after been blocked once

In rare cases, blocking an employee's anonymized reporting for 6 months might not be enough .

HR BP can then suspend their access to anonymized features for a year.

But the employee keeps the option to send a complaint (not anonymized) to HR.

from outside

  • Anonymised feedbacks and reports can be sent only within the company.
