Volantary

1Educative Preperation

Light plea bargain-equivalent with no strings attached

HR can recommend to the wrongdoer to voluntarily perform a self-educative action, which is also restorative for the reporter.

HR may choose to offer the alleged wrongdoer one of the below

  • 1. A handwritten, circumstantiated letter of apology.
  • 2. a three-page narrative essay entitled "Working with a Colleague with Toxic Behavior".
  • 3. One to three counseling sessions by a certified specialist

These are strictly voluntary. An employer does not have the right to impose them to an employee

Each of these items, especially the last two, has been shown in the laboratory to have a cognitive/educational effect.

2Encourage Bystander Intervention (colleagues)

Focusing on bystanders rather than wrongdoers is efficient to pushback against toxicity,

(Harvard Business Review may 2020)

A bystander can either directly send an anonymized feedback or an anonymized report to HR

OR offers to join the victim if she decides to send a feedback or a report

Mandatory Games
