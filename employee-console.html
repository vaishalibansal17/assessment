4Shield against anonymised abuse

Anonymity is the solution for employees to speak up despite their fear it may tempt abusers.

Our Shield blocks abusers by denying them access to the Anonymized features

BUT

  • It never denies access to the “complaint to HR” feature, since it is not anonymized
  • …and always keeps a door opened for employees who were misunderstanding

4.1Automated safeguard anti-serial sender of
Anonymized Feedbacks

This is a typical case of misunderstanding
of what is toxic behavior

If an employee sends many feedbacks different
persons it might be a misuse
...or something else entirely

It can be either:

  • An abusive sender
  • or a contributor surrounded by abusive colleagues

In both cases, HR should know.

So it’s best to let the employee calm down and learn more about toxicity, while not allowing her/him to send feedback for 3 months.

But, in case those many feedbacks are sent because of a case of toxic environment, this employee is also encouraged to report to HR

2 steps algorithm
for safeguard anti-serial sender abuse

STEP1CASE

Triggers:

A contributor sends feedbacks to 4 colleagues within 2 months.

STEP2 Shield

The Sender:
  • Can't send another feedback during 3 month
  • Is invited to send anonymized Report to HR Reply

4.2Automated safeguard anti-group
coordinated feedbacks sending

Being several to send abusive feedbacks to someone could seem to guarantee impunity

If several colleagues send feedback to the
the same employee, it might be a complot
...or something else entirely

It can be either:

  • A specially abusive contributor
  • Or a collective complot against some in both cases: HR must know

So HR BP must be automatically alerted (either to help the victim of a complot or to reign in a really toxic employee).

HR BP can easily and discreetly gauge this employee’s work environnement climate, using the app.’s “Short Local Toxicity Survey”

2 steps algorithm
for safeguard anti-coordinated sender abuse

STEP1CASE

Triggers:

A contributor received feedbacks from
4 colleagues or more within 2 months

STEP2 Shield

The Sender:
  • No more feedbacks are sent to the receiver.
  • HR is informed of trouble in this part of the company.
  • With name of the feedback recipient but not of senders.
  • Suggesting a Targeted Toxicity Survey

Safeguard anti-Anonymized Report to
HR abuse

Sending a fake anonymized report to HR might seem tempting and guaranteeing impunity. But the app. gives tools to HR to investigate (Short local toxicity survey, Anonymized chat with reporter...)
...and to sanction abuse

If an employee continues to send
unsubstantiated anonymized reports to HR
after been blocked once

In rare cases, blocking an employee's anonymized reports for 6 months might not be enough .

HR BP can then suspend his/her access to anonymized features for a year

But the employee keeps the option to send a complaint (not anonymized) to HR.
