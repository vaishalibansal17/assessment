HR Console

Most companies have a help-line or other system to report harassment/discrimination cases to HR

But HR lacks of tools to resolve the reported cases, especially those of mild daily toxicity, which fuels attrition the most..

HR can mostly use :

  • Settlement or firing, which is costly and further attrition
  • or generalized Diversity, Equity and Inclusion training, which doesn't address individual cases

Our console gives tools to HR to investigate and then resolve individual cases and thus contribute to reduce attrition

6. Action tools

Non-disciplinary Cognitive Behavioral tools help in correcting behavior through voluntary and mandatory actions. In the case that these don’t work, disciplinary tools can be used.

Follow-survey tools help HR assess the results of his/her actions
