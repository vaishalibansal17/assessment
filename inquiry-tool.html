Inquiry Tools

Inquiry Tools empower HR to conduct investigations while easily preserving the anonymity of employees

  • 1Anonymized Chat
  • 2Targeted Toxicity Survey
  • 3Toxicity Interview Preparation
  • 4Pre-written messaging at inquiry’s closure

The starting point: for HR
an already documented report with evidence

When the Anonymized Toxicity Feedback hasn’t been sufficient, it can be easily converted into an Anonymized Toxicity Report to HR

1 Anonymized chat with reporter

The reporter feels secure because he/she can respond to HR without revealing his/her identity

2 Targeted Toxicity Survey of the
colleagues of alleged wrongdoer

An easy way to check the credibility of an anonymous report:

Just enter a handful of email addresses of the alleged wrongdoer's colleagues

The app will survey them about their work environment

  • without naming the alleged wrongdoer
  • and anonymizing their answers

3.1 Pre-written draft mail to alleged wrongdoer,
preparing for an interview

(documented, and with form to fill)

Informing an employee that he/she’s accused of toxic behavior might be tricky

JustEase provides:

  • Carefully pre-written draft mail easy to use because you won’t have to write it from scratch each time
  • Information from the Anonymous toxicity the report is added to the mail

3.2Preparing a toxicity interview

1. The employee responds to the toxicity report (using a scale to avoid knee-jerk denial)

2. The employee fills the Targeted Workplace Toxicity Survey as his/her colleagues (without knowing their answers)

3. He/she can show goodwill by agreeing advance to voluntarily use some of the HR action tools to combat toxicity (Acceptance would be a huge step for HR)

3.3Toxicity Interview Dashboard

When interviewing the alleged wrongdoer, HR BP can rely on JustEase’s summary displaying all results of his inquiry:

1. Toxicity report to HR

2. Alleged wrongdoer response to the pre-interview questionnaire

3. Alleged wrongdoer’s answer to the Targeted Workplace Toxicity survey

4. Actions he/she agreed in advance to do (such as interactive game...etc.)

5. HR BP can add his/her comments on the case

4Pre-written messaging to reporter and alleged
wrongdoer at inquiry’s closure

Here, message wording is a challenge for HR

Because HR BP has to tell:

  • to wrongdoer that he/she is wrongdoing and that correcting actions will be taken (if inquiry is conclusive) OR
  • To reporter that he/she is wrong (if inquiry is inconclusive)

JustEase makes it easier by offering HR BP to start with carefully pre-written messages (manually editable)
